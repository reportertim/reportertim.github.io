First Coast Brewery Data Timothy Gibbons/Jacksonville Business Journal 2023-12-22

Overview

As of December 22, 2023, there are 345 breweries with current licenses in the state of Florida, dating back to Yuengling Brewing Company Of Tampa Inc, which received its license on April 14, 1999.

This data updates automatically when the script is run, but it can be manually downloaded here.

On the First Coast1, there are 33 breweries with current licenses. The first, Bold City Brewery Llc, was issued its license on an original date of September 08, 2008.

An embeddable version of this chart can be found here.

Growth

The number of breweries across the state has been steadily rising. The most recent brewery to be licensed in Florida is Perfect Plain Brewing Company in Pensacola, which was permitted on December 18, 2023.

On the First Coast, the most recent permit was issued to Bedlam Brewing Company in Jacksonville on an effective date of November 21, 2023.

Sales

The state collects data (for taxation purposes) on direct sales by breweries – generally speaking, these are sales made in a taproom. (Unfortunately, overall sales numbers, showing sales through the brewer-wholesaler-retailer system, are not available.)

This data must be downloaded manually; the most recent information was pulled December 22, 2023. The data, which is split between breweries and brewpubs, can be found here.

Breweries across the state obviously saw those sales collapse during the height of the Covid-19 pandemic. The First Coast has rebounded more than the state as a whole.

Embeddable versions of the Florida chart is here and of the First Coast chart is here

The brewery in Florida that has sold the most beer directly to consumers so far in 2023 is 3 Daughters Brewing Llc, which has sold 71,554.1 gallons.

On the First Coast, Wicked Barley’s taproom moved the largest amount of beer, selling 32,312.36 gallons directly to consumers.

In the latest 12 months of data – from November 2022 to October 2023 – the 33 breweries and brewpubs in the area that reported sales moved 328,317.2 gallons of beer.
