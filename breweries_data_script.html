First Coast Brewery Data Timothy Gibbons/Jacksonville Business Journal 2024-07-01

Overview

As of July 01, 2024, there are 360 breweries with current licenses in the state of Florida, dating back to Yuengling Brewing Company Of Tampa Inc, which received its license on April 14, 1999.

This data updates automatically when the script is run, but it can be manually downloaded here.

On the First Coast1, there are 35 breweries with current licenses. The first, Bold City Brewery Llc, was issued its license on an original date of September 08, 2008.

An embeddable version of this chart can be found here.

Growth

The number of breweries across the state has been steadily rising. The most recent brewery to be licensed in Florida is Ivanhoe Park Brewing Co in Orlando, which was permitted on June 26, 2024.

On the First Coast, the most recent permit was issued to Coquina Coast Brewing Company in Flagler Beach on an effective date of June 25, 2024.

Sales

The state collects data (for taxation purposes) on direct sales by breweries – generally speaking, these are sales made in a taproom. (Unfortunately, overall sales numbers, showing sales through the brewer-wholesaler-retailer system, are not available.)

This data must be downloaded manually; the most recent information was pulled July 01, 2024. The data, which is split between breweries and brewpubs, can be found here.

Breweries across the state obviously saw those sales collapse during the height of the Covid-19 pandemic. Right after the pandemic, the First Coast rebounded more than the state as a whole.

Embeddable versions of the Florida chart is here and of the First Coast chart is here

The brewery in Florida that has sold the most beer directly to consumers in 2023 is Anheuser Busch, which has sold 82,826.74 gallons.

Among First Coast craft breweries — excluding Budweiser — Wicked Barley’s taproom moved the largest amount of beer, selling 37,752.86 gallons directly to consumers.

In the latest 12 months of data – from June 2023 to May 2024 – the 35 breweries and brewpubs in the area that reported sales moved 331,027.5 gallons of beer.
